About Tutedude

TD is an online learning platform founded by IIT alumni that offers self-paced courses in technology and other fields like digital marketing and UI/UX design. It is known for its affordable courses, with a unique 100% refund policy upon completion. Key features include 1:1 live doubt-solving, personal mentorship, resume building, and sometimes internship opportunities. Course offerings and features Course variety: Offers a range of self-paced courses in areas like web development, data science, UI/UX design, and digital marketing. 100% refund: Provides a full refund on the enrollment fee for completing a course within a specific timeframe, making it a "risk-free" learning model. Personalized support: Includes 1:1 live doubt-solving sessions and personal mentorship to assist learners throughout their studies. Practical learning: Emphasizes hands-on projects, with instructors coding along, to help students build a portfolio and understand project development. Additional tools: Provides a resume builder and offers combo packs for multiple courses at discounted prices. Additional benefits Gamification: Uses a reward-based motivation system to encourage course completion. Community: Has a large community of over 100,000 students. Internship opportunities: Offers remote internship opportunities to eligible students upon course completion.
